Administrative Assistant

We are looking for an experienced Administrative Assistant to provide a well-organised supportive work to our whole team and the commercial team in particular. You are passionate about overseeing and structuring the organisation’s activities and plan with foresight. You focus on solutions instead of the problem, and look for effective ways to handle whatever comes your way.

You embrace team play and are keen on new challenges in a startup environment. We are tech savvy, you should be too.

The position is based in Budapest, Hungary.

Your key responsibilities:

  • Develop, implement, maintain and monitor administrative and filing systems, procedures and policies.
  • Oversee, coordinate and monitor our sales team in their acquisition efforts.
  • Oversee, coordinate and monitor our content tagging team.
  • Handle incoming support request and queries by our clients via Zendesk.
  • Prepare the required documentation for our accountants and supervise all accounting related matters.
  • Support our product team in their pursuit to improve our product by contacting selected consumer for customer interviews.
  • Manage all human resources related matters.
  • Oversee and execute payout to suppliers and business partners.
  • Produce reports to support decision making processes.
  • Analyze and improve operating practices throughout the whole organisation.
  • Maintain our office infrastructure and supply.
  • Resolve ad hoc administrative tasks.

Required skills and experience:

  • At least 2 years of hands-on experience as project or office manager.
  • Effective organisation and time management skills.
  • Ability to multitask and prioritise daily workload.
  • Knowledge of project and office management systems and procedures.
  • Excellent verbal, written and in-person communication skills in English.
